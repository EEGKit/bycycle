bycycle.burst.twothresh_amp¶

bycycle.burst.twothresh_amp(x, Fs, f_range, amp_threshes, N_cycles_min=3, magnitude_type='amplitude', return_amplitude=False, filter_kwargs=None)¶

Detect periods of oscillatory bursting in a neural signal by using two amplitude thresholds.

Parameters:
  • x (array-like 1d) – voltage time series
  • Fs (float) – The sampling rate in Hz
  • f_range (tuple (low, high), Hz) – frequency range for oscillator of interest
  • amp_threshes (tuple (low, high)) – Threshold values for determining timing of bursts. These values are in units of amplitude (or power, if specified) normalized to the median amplitude (value 1).
  • N_cycles_min (float) – minimum burst duration in terms of number of cycles of f_range[0]
  • magnitude_type (string in ('power', 'amplitude')) – metric of magnitude used for thresholding
  • filter_kwargs (dict) – keyword arguments to filt.bandpass_filter
